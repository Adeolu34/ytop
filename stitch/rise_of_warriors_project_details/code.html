Rise of Warriors (ROW)

Unleashing the potential within. A high-energy journey to cultivate resilience, emotional intelligence, and leadership in the next generation of changemakers.

Forging Resilient Leaders

Rise of Warriors (ROW) is not just a seminar; it is an awakening. Designed as a high-intensity, community-focused program, ROW cultivates resilience, leadership, and personal development in young people.

Our mission is to empower the next generation to face life's challenges head-on. Through immersive workshops and mentorship, we equip participants with the tools to turn obstacles into stepping stones for success.

Journey Through the Versions

From our humble beginnings to a nationwide movement, trace the evolution of the warrior spirit.

ROW 1.0: The Awakening

2017

The first gathering at University of Ibadan. 50 students, one classroom, and a spark that started a fire.

ROW 3.0: Expansion

2019

Expanding to three new campuses. The curriculum evolved to include intense leadership workshops.

ROW 5.0: The Digital Pivot
