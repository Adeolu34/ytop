General

What is YTOP Global? expand_more

YTOP Global is a youth empowerment non-governmental organization dedicated to nurturing the next generation of leaders. We provide mentorship, community support, and educational opportunities to help young people realize their full potential.

Where is YTOP Global located? expand_more

While our headquarters are based in Lagos, Nigeria, we operate globally through virtual programs and local chapters in various countries. Our reach extends to youth across Africa and beyond.

Volunteering

How can I become a volunteer? expand_more

You can become a volunteer by filling out the application form on our "Get Involved" page. We recruit volunteers periodically for various roles including event planning, mentorship, content creation, and community management.

Are there remote volunteering opportunities? expand_more

Yes! Many of our programs are coordinated online, and we welcome volunteers from all over the world to contribute remotely. Skills in digital marketing, graphic design, and research are highly valued for remote roles.

Donations

Is my donation tax-deductible? expand_more

YTOP Global is a registered non-profit organization. Donations may be tax-deductible depending on your country's tax laws. We provide a receipt for every donation which you can use for your records and tax filing.

How are donations used? expand_more

Your donations go directly towards funding our youth development programs, such as the Rise of Warriors mentorship program, educational workshops, and community outreach initiatives. We maintain transparency in our financial reports.

diversity_3

Rise of Warriors

What is the Rise of Warriors program? expand_more

Rise of Warriors is our flagship mentorship and leadership development program designed for ambitious young people. It combines intensive training, personalized mentorship, and real-world project experience to build the next generation of changemakers.

Who is eligible to apply? expand_more

The program is open to youth aged 18-30 who demonstrate a passion for social impact and leadership. We look for individuals who are committed to personal growth and contributing positively to their communities.
